About Kaltura Player
The Kaltura player SDK enables seamless video playback across browsers and many platforms with support for advanced streaming formats. The Kaltura Player based on our open source Player integrates a core library, UI framework, HLS & MPEG-DASH capabilities, ads and monetization, and a variety of analytics options.
